README

Scripts in this folder process data in 

OneDrive/Oyster Biosensor (or Shellfish Science Hub?)/2024 JEL field 

and

OneDrive/Oyster Biosensor (or Shellfish Science Hub?)/2024 JEL lab

JEL lab was a greenhouse experiment in the tanks with 24 oysters hooked up to biosensors.

JEL field was an intertidal/subtidal experiment off the right side of the JEL dock, on the riprap, with 6 oysters under rockweed, 6 oysters on bare intertidal substrate, and 12 oysters in the subtidal.
